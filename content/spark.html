--- title: "Spark" ---

Are you interested in contributing a talk to the DataScience@UL-FRI Spark Sessions? Fill out the form below and apply!

Talks must be between 5 and 7 minutes long. Topics include, but are not limited to, showcasing your company, project, insights, best practices, methods, or software. We will prioritize talks based on interest to the broader data science (AI, machine learning, business analytics, IT) community.

We review applications on a rolling basis and typically respond within 2 weeks.
